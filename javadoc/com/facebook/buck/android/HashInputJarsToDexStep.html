    SmartDexingStep.DexInputHashesProvider, Step

    public class HashInputJarsToDexStep
extends AbstractExecutionStep
implements SmartDexingStep.DexInputHashesProvider
    Step responsible for hashing dex inputs to be passed to SmartDexingStep. It goes through each dex input, looks at the classes that went into that jar, and uses the class hashes collected in AndroidPackageableCollection to calculate the final hash of the input.
