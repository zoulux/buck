        public static MavenUberJar create(JavaLibrary rootRule,
                  BuildRuleParams params,
                  SourcePathResolver resolver,
                  com.google.common.base.Optional<String> mavenCoords)
        Will traverse transitive dependencies of rootRule, separating those that do and don't have maven coordinates. Those that do will be considered maven-external dependencies. They will be returned by getMavenDeps() and will end up being specified as dependencies in pom.xml. Others will be packaged in the same jar as if they are just a part of the one published item.
